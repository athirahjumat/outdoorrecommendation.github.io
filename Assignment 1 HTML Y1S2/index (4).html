Singapore Sports Hub

Get out of your house and enjoy quality time with your loved ones at Singapore Sports Hub, one of our very own hearts of outdoor activities. Don't be afraid to explore the different venues within the parameters of Singapore Sports Hub.

Visit their Outdoor Volleyball Court

Be sure to try out their outdoor volleyball court with your loved ones, as you experience the real sensation of "Beach Volleyball" without being at the actual beach! The sand pitch will also allow you to play volleyball with your loved ones without wearing shoes. However do take note that balls are not provided, so remember to bring your own balls!

Visit their River Side Walk

Conveniently located outside of Kallang Wave Mall, you can enjoy the sceneric view of the people kayaking on the water bodies and our beautiful buildings while having a small picnic with your loved ones! Remember to bring insect or mosquito repellent so that you wont get bitten by bugs! The windy and park-like atmosphere will definitely make you come back! In case you run out of snacks and drinks or maybe even suddenly decide to have an impromptu picnic plan with your loved ones, Kallang Wave Mall just behind will provide you with all the things you need. Snacks or picnic mat from the supermarket, or even a hefty meal take-away meal to enjoy, you name it, its all so convenient!

Visit their SplashnSurf

Enjoy your off days with loved ones, especially your kids or younger siblings at the SplashnSurf located directly above Kallang Wave Mall @ Level 3. Countless number of fun slides and facilities provided for not only the younger ones but also yourself. Remember! Age is just a number, so don't be afraid to let your inner child out. As long as you are having fun, no one can judge.

Visit their 100PLUS Promenade

Singapore Sports Hub provides visitors with three coloured lanes that stretches 888 metres. And thats not it! For the whole of the stretch, it is fully sheltered so you and your loved ones could go for a jog or even a fun race together without having to worry about the notorious and unpredictable weather we have in Singapore these days!
